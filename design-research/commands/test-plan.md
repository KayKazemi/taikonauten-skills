# /test-plan

Design a comprehensive usability test plan.

## Steps

1. Use the `usability-test-plan` skill to create the full test plan for $ARGUMENTS — research questions, methodology, participant criteria, tasks, metrics, and facilitation guide.
2. If personas are available, align tasks to primary persona scenarios.
3. Include a pilot test checklist.

## Output

Complete usability test plan with research questions, tasks, success metrics, participant criteria, facilitation guide, and pilot checklist.

Consider following up with `/design-research:synthesize` to help analyse results after testing, or `/design-research:interview` to prepare follow-up interview scripts.
