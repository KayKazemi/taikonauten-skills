# /scope-project

Define and document project scope in full.

## Steps

1. **Brief analysis**: Use the `client-brief` skill to confirm objectives, constraints, and stakeholders for $ARGUMENTS.
2. **Scope definition**: Use the `project-scoping` skill to produce a complete scope document — deliverables, in/out of scope, milestones, assumptions, and dependencies.
3. **Revision rounds**: Specify the number of revision rounds included per deliverable and the feedback management approach.
4. **Feedback process**: Use the `feedback-management` skill to define the review structure, approval workflow, and change request process.
5. **Risk log**: Identify the top 3–5 scope risks and the mitigation for each.
6. **Client sign-off**: Produce a scope summary formatted for client review and sign-off.

## Output

Signed-off scope document including deliverables, in/out of scope, milestone schedule, assumptions, revision rounds, feedback process, and risk log.

Consider following up with `/client-pitch:write-proposal` to formalise the scope into a deliverable proposal, or `/design-ops:plan-sprint` to translate scope into sprint planning.
