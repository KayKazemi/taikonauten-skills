# /run-kickoff

Prepare everything needed for a successful project kickoff.

## Steps

1. **Brief review**: Use the `client-brief` skill to confirm the project's objectives, success criteria, and open questions for $ARGUMENTS.
2. **Scope confirmation**: Use the `project-scoping` skill to review deliverables, milestones, and dependencies before the meeting.
3. **Kickoff agenda**: Use the `kickoff-facilitation` skill to build a structured agenda tailored to the project's complexity and the client's familiarity with the process.
4. **Stakeholder preparation**: Identify all attendees, their roles, and what each person needs from the kickoff.
5. **Kickoff materials**: Prepare a concise pre-read (brief summary, scope, agenda) to circulate 48 hours before.
6. **Post-kickoff notes**: After the meeting, consolidate decisions, open items, action items with owners, and the first confirmed milestone.

## Output

Kickoff agenda, pre-read document, and post-kickoff notes template with action items and next milestone confirmation.

Consider following up with `/client-pitch:scope-project` to formalise any scope decisions made during the kickoff.
