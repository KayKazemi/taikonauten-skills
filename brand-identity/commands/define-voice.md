# /define-voice

Build a complete brand voice and messaging framework.

## Steps

1. **Brand foundation**: Confirm or define the brand's personality, values, and audience using the `brand-strategy` skill for $ARGUMENTS.
2. **Voice pillars**: Use the `brand-voice` skill to define 3–4 voice characteristics with examples and anti-examples.
3. **Tone mapping**: Define how tone shifts across key contexts — marketing, onboarding, error states, support, social.
4. **Messaging hierarchy**: Write the tagline, elevator pitch (sentence / paragraph / page), and key messages by audience segment.
5. **Vocabulary guide**: Define words/phrases to use, avoid, and the right level of technical language per audience.
6. **Writing examples**: Produce 5 real-world writing samples (e.g., a homepage headline, an error message, a social post, an email subject line, a push notification) that demonstrate the voice in practice.

## Output

Brand voice guide with pillars, tone map, messaging hierarchy, vocabulary list, and annotated writing examples.

Consider following up with `/designer-toolkit:write-rationale` to document the strategic reasoning behind voice decisions.
